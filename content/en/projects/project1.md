## Overview

Designed multiple business AI tools on a low-code AI platform while iterating on the existing intelligent customer service system to improve operational efficiency and conversion rates.

## My Role

AI Product Manager — Requirements Analysis, Solution Design, Iteration

## Approach

1. **AI Tool Design**: Identified pain points in real estate operations and independently designed AI Skills for listing copy generation, property highlight analysis, and batch processing
2. **Cost Optimization**: Introduced code nodes to replace repetitive LLM calls, encapsulated reusable business interfaces
3. **Customer Service Iteration**: Optimized prompt engineering and response logic for traditional rule-based customer service

## Results

- Positive improvement in customer service lead conversion rate
- Cross-scenario skill reuse with reduced model call costs
- Quantifiable business value delivery
